Frequently Asked Question

How does the Lenditt Application work?

To begin with, download the Lenditt Application from the Play Store or the Apple App Store, depending on your device. Once installed, create a unique profile. After registration, fill in your primary and employment details, serving as proof. Following this, choose from the Loan offer and click to apply.

How many types of loans does Lenditt provide?
Lenditt Loans are Categorised into 6 distinct types. They are
  • Instant Personal Loan
  • Instant Medical Loan
  • Instant Travel Loan
  • Instant Bike Loan
  • Instant Mobile Loan
  • Instant Consumer Durable Loan
What are the steps to apply for a Loan from Lenditt?
Step 1: Get Started- Download the Lenditt App and Sign up. step 2:Eligibility Check- Submit Basic details & Documents to check eligibility. step 3:KYC & Banking- Upload Documents for KYC and Bank Account Details. step 4:Loan Selection- Select from the eligible Loan Offers available. step 5:Access Funds- Check your Bank for the loan.
Can I apply for a loan with a low credit score?
Yes, you can apply for a loan with a low credit score.
How is the loan amount disbursed?
The loan amount is directly deposited into the applicant's bank account without involving third parties, digital wallets, cash transactions, or other payment platforms.
What repayment methods are available?
Repayment can be made via UPI, Debit Card, Credit Card, or Net Banking.
Do I have to pay processing fees or hidden charges while applying for the loan?
Processing fees start from 6.5% of the loan amount (depending upon your risk profile). There are no hidden charges.
